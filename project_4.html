Auditorium in Benitatxell

Benitatxell, Spain | 2016 | #Competition #Facilities
Team: Antonio Altarriba, Raimon Espasa Bou, David LÃ³pez

It is a contest requested by the City of benitatxell with the aim of building a new auditorium with cultural spaces in the city. The location was very tight for the entire program to be asked but still, we managed to introduce each of the necessary uses.

The building is divided into three bands that are arranged according to the privacy of use, privacy smaller spaces cafeteria and exhibitions, a little more private area is the auditorium and finally applications dressing rooms and back stage.

Moreover, the auditorium is incorporated a window that opens to experior to interpret different functions face an audience abroad.
