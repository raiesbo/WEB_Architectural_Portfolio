Rural hotel

Requena, Spain | 2013 | #AcademicProject #Facility

The idea of this project is the development of a hotel with geometry that resembles traditional housing, but with a modern conception.

The project consists of three volumes and a basement. Two of which provide solutions to housing needs and areas of day as a meeting rooms and all kinds of activities taking place. While the third, individually, is responsible for conferences, administration, the dining area and tastings.
