Hafenstraße 8 | 1st Prize

Wittenberge, Germany | 2017 | #Competition #Residential
rundzwei Architekten
Team: Marc Dufour-Feronce, Raimon Espasa Bou, Ana Domenti, Andreas Reeg

The multi-family house Hafenstraße 8 creates a new entrance situation for the transition between the historical old town and the former industrial area and is arranged on the competition area in such a way that a plaza as an entrance area to the river promenade is created.

This connects the visitor streams from Zollstraße, Hafenstraße and riverfront promenade and opens and divides, over the new building, the transition from the old town to the former industrial estate.

Each individual floor consist of at least three apartments. All the apartments have a main orientation of the living areas to the river Elbe and have an affiliation to the living area, which is partly a balcony/loggia, offering to the inhabitants a combination of the protected privacy of a loggia and the panoramic view of a balcony.
